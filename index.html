About Us

The Department of Software Engineering, School of Information and Communication Technology, Federal University of Technology Owerri, is committed to producing world-class software engineers equipped with knowledge, innovation, and practical problem-solving skills.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading hub for software innovation, research, and education in Africa, preparing graduates to shape the digital future.

Mission

To train highly skilled software engineers with a balance of theory, practice, and ethical responsibility to solve global challenges.

Our History

Established in 2019, the department has been at the forefront of advancing computing education and innovation in Nigeria.
